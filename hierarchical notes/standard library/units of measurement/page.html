• Dimensional Analysis
    • Automatically compute the resulting unit from the units participating in an expression (e.g. speed[double [m/s]] = walk_distance[double [m]] / time_taken[double [s]].
    • Automatically make required conversions between units of different systems of measurement measuring the same quantity (e.g. yards to meters) and expand prefixes (e.g. k to 1000).
    • Issue errors when incompatible units are mixed (e.g. 2m + 4.2kg).
  • Units
    • metric units (e.g. gram (g), ampere (A), metre (m), °C, etc.)
    • currencies (e.g. $, €, ¤)
    • indivisible items (e.g. 3 apples, 4 arrays, 5 elements)
    • information (bit, byte, etc.)
  • Prefixes
    • metric (e.g. k,M,G,T, etc.)
    • binary (ki,Mi,Gi,Ti, etc.)
